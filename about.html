About & FAQ

ABOUT THE WORK

Who's behind the design

Going Beyond is a one-person DTP and graphic design studio based in Burari, Delhi NCR. Every order is designed personally — there is no team, no outsourcing, and no back-and-forth between departments.

The focus is on print-ready and platform-ready design work: posters and banners, YouTube thumbnails, packaging design, and full book layout and formatting. Work is trained through a certified DTP program and built through hands-on client projects across posters, study material, and book design.

Every order is treated as a print job: clear specifications in, a clean file out, on the timeline that was promised.

BEFORE YOU ORDER

Frequently asked questions

How do I pay?

Payment is made by UPI. Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a QR code and payment details on the order or payment page.

How long does a design take?

Most single designs (posters, thumbnails, packaging) are delivered within 24–48 hours of confirmation. Book layout projects are scheduled individually based on page count.

Can I ask for changes after I see the design?

Yes. Reasonable revisions are included before the design is marked complete. Changes requested after final delivery may be treated as a new order.

Do you take orders from outside Delhi NCR?

Yes. All designs are delivered as digital files, so location does not matter. Only physical printing or delivery would require a local pickup, and that is not part of this service.

How do I check my order status?

Use the Track Order page with the Order ID you received after placing your order.

POLICIES

Refund policy & terms

Refund policy

Payment confirms an order and reserves design time for it. Because of this:

  • A full refund is available only if the design work has not yet started.
  • Once design work has started, the order is non-refundable, regardless of the stage it is in.
  • If an order cannot be completed due to an error on our side, a full refund will be issued.

Revisions

Each order includes a reasonable number of revisions to match the brief you provided at the time of ordering. Requests that go beyond the original brief (a different design concept, a different size, or additional pages) may be quoted as a new order.

Ownership & usage rights

Once an order is paid for and delivered, you own the rights to use the final design for your stated purpose (printing, posting, publishing).

Going Beyond retains the right to display completed work in the portfolio and on this website, unless you request otherwise in writing before the order is delivered.

Copyright

Any reference images, fonts, or brand assets you provide must be your own or properly licensed for use. Going Beyond is not responsible for copyright issues arising from material supplied by the client.

By placing an order, you confirm that you have read and agree to this Refund Policy and these Terms.
